# armgin 0.2.0

* Summarise by individual grouping variables one at a time with `method
    = "individual"`.  The argument `hierarchy` is deprecated and replaced by
    `method`, which can be one of  `"hierarchy"`, `"combination"` or
    `"individual"`.
